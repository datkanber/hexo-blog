## Hi, I'm Burak Kanber

I'm a **Digital Transformation Specialist** working at the intersection of software engineering, industrial IoT, and applied AI — currently contributing to the [MATISSE EU Horizon Europe KDT Project](https://matisse-kdt.eu/) at Eskisehir Osmangazi University.

I architect cloud platforms that bring together academic research models — from FMI/FMU co-simulation to ROS 2 telemetry and 3D Digital Twins — into unified, deployable web ecosystems. I also develop real-time computer vision systems (YOLOv8-based defect detection) and coordinate R&D efforts within the EU Horizon Europe programme.
